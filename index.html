Course Overview

This cohort course is designed for individuals with little or no background in programming and computer science. The curriculum covers the essential concepts and techniques required to become a proficient programmer, as well as providing a solid foundation in computer science. Participants will gain hands-on experience in Python, one of the most popular programming languages, while also learning about algorithms, data structures, and software development principles.
